Summary

We operate a review platform. Reviews are anonymous by default. We collect limited technical information to operate the service, maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve reliability. Optional sign-in features may store basic account information. We do not sell personal information.

1) Information we collect

We collect information in three categories: (a) information you submit, (b) information collected automatically when you use the service, and (c) information associated with optional account features.

  • Content you submit: ratings, written comments, selected teacher/course, and related timestamps.
  • Basic technical data: limited device and browser signals (such as an anonymous device identifier, user agent, and similar operational metadata), and network information (such as an IP address or a privacy-preserving representation of it), to support security, fraud prevention, and service integrity.
  • Usage events: high-level activity events (e.g., page views and actions) used for reliability, troubleshooting, analytics, and abuse prevention.
  • Optional account information: if you choose to sign in, we store information necessary to create and maintain your account and provide account-related features.
Do not submit sensitive personal information. Please do not post student names, IDs, phone numbers, addresses, schedules, private communications, or any other information that could identify a private individual. Content submitted is user-generated and may be visible to others.

2) How we use information

We use collected information to operate, maintain, secure, and improve the service. This includes displaying content, calculating aggregates, detecting and preventing abuse, debugging, measuring performance, and enforcing service rules. We do not use information to build cross-site advertising profiles.

Operate the service

Process and display ratings and comments, maintain platform functionality, and support user features.

Protect integrity

Limit spam, automated activity, manipulation, and other misuse to preserve reliability and fairness.

We may also use aggregated, de-identified, or otherwise non-identifying information for analytics and reporting to understand usage trends and improve the platform.

3) Anonymity and identifiers

Reviews are anonymous by default. The service may associate a review with limited technical signals (for example, an anonymous device identifier and network information) to support security, prevent abuse, and enforce rate limits. These signals are used for operational purposes and are not intended to publicly identify a reviewer.

4) Accounts and Google sign-in

Creating an account is optional. If you choose to sign in with Google, we receive and store basic account information needed to authenticate you and maintain your account (such as your name, email address, and a Google account identifier). We do not receive your Google password.

Account features may include account-based actions or preferences. You can use the service without signing in.

5) Moderation and enforcement

The platform may use a combination of automated and manual processes to support service integrity, enforce rules, and respond to reports of misuse. Actions may include limiting visibility, restricting functionality, removing content, or taking other steps consistent with the Terms.

Moderation decisions may consider content signals and limited technical context. We do not guarantee that all content will be reviewed or that any specific moderation outcome will occur.

6) Sharing, service providers, and legal requests

No sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information and do not share personal information with third parties for targeted advertising.

We may share information with vendors and service providers that help operate the platform (for example, hosting, content delivery, analytics, and security services). These providers are permitted to process information only to provide services to us and are expected to protect it consistent with applicable obligations.

We may disclose information if we believe it is reasonably necessary to comply with applicable law, respond to valid legal process, protect the safety, integrity, or rights of the service and users, investigate abuse, or prevent fraud and security incidents.

7) Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8) Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, enforce the Terms, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and protect the platform from misuse. Retention periods may vary based on the type of information and operational needs. Certain logs may be retained for limited periods for security and troubleshooting.

9) Your rights and choices

You can use the service without creating an account. If you choose to sign in, you may stop signing in at any time. Where applicable, you may request access, correction, or deletion of certain information, subject to legal and operational limitations (for example, security logs, integrity controls, and content that must be retained for compliance or platform safety).

California residents may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws. We respond to requests as required by law and may need to verify your request before acting on it.

Practical note: Because reviews are anonymous by default, we may be unable to reliably verify authorship of specific anonymous submissions without sufficient supporting information.

10) Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “effective” date is the date the policy is posted. Your continued use of the service after an update becomes effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy to the extent permitted by law.

11) Contact

Questions or requests related to privacy should be submitted through the contact options available on the site. If you are reporting a security issue, please include enough detail for us to investigate responsibly.
