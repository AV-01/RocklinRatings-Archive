About the “legal threat” rumor

This page provides clarity on what we have and have not seen, what to look for in legitimate communications, and how to keep the platform focused and credible.

Updated: January 14, 2026 Anonymous by default Privacy Terms

What’s happening

A message has been circulating that claims there is a “legal threat” related to Rocklin Ratings. As of now, we have not seen any communication that appears to be an official district notice or a formal legal notice.

If you encounter a screenshot, banner, or “notice” that is vague, emotionally written, or missing verifiable details, treat it with skepticism. Legitimate communications are specific, verifiable, and clearly identify who is issuing them.

Anonymity & privacy

Rocklin Ratings is built to be anonymous by default. We do not require users to post under their real names, and the platform is designed to minimize the collection and exposure of personal identifiers.

Like any platform, anonymity is not an absolute guarantee in every conceivable scenario. If valid legal process is ever presented, we will respond in accordance with our published policies and applicable law.

Keeping reviews focused

Rocklin Ratings exists to help students make informed decisions about classes and academic experiences. To keep the platform available and credible, we are updating and enforcing our Terms of Service.

  • No profanity or personal attacks. Reviews should describe experiences, not target individuals.
  • No harassment or threats. Content intended to intimidate, insult, or provoke is not permitted.
  • No private or identifying information. Do not include student names, schedules, IDs, contact details, or private communications.
  • Be specific and constructive. Workload, grading, clarity, pacing, and expectations are appropriate topics.

Reviews that violate these standards may be removed, and repeated abuse of the platform may result in restrictions under our Terms.

Affiliation

Rocklin Ratings is an independent project and is not an official school resource. It is not endorsed by any school, district, or public agency. Reviews reflect user opinions and are not official evaluations.

If you see something concerning

If you see a “notice” that appears fabricated or designed to alarm people, do not spread it further. If any content on this site violates our rules, report it through the contact page and we will review it.
